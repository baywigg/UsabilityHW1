Username Guidelines

  • Must be between10and12characters
  • First character must be lowercase betweena-h
  • Must contain at least two letters (one uppercase), two numbers, and two special characters
  • Last two characters must be numbers, where the second last number is even and the last number is odd
  • No vowels allowed after the3rdcharacter

Password Guidelines

  • Must be exactly16characters
  • First character must be a special character but not the ones used in the username
  • The fourth and twelfth characters must be uppercase letters.
  • Must contain at least1uppercase letter,1lowercase letter,1number, and2special characters
